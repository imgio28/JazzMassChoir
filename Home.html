JAZZ MASS CHOIR

About us

During that time cursillo movement was very active. I was the moderator of the Jr cursillitas. Being the.moderator. I was thinking of a project that had something to do with the church. A project where the young members would be very interested as well in stead of putting their attention on DRUGS. I gathered some members who were musically inclined but instesd of doing the Gregorian chant wherein they made use of the traditionsl organ we decided to use the guitar para mabago sa pandinig. At that time we had a lot of members that were in college and popular ang folk songs. Kaya nga we had been singing songs from different colleges. Tawag nga sa atin noon Guitar Mass, folk Mass and ultimately became Jazz Mass kasi we had been singing popular songs Nina Peter Paul and Mary , sebastian temple and other popular singers. Later on we introduced the songs in Tagalog At first when we talked to Fr. Thys . He was hesitant to allow us to sing in the regular Sunday Mass because he was worried the Manangs may not like the idea of changing the typical Mass to the tune of the guitar. So we entered into an agreement that Jazz Mass will do the anticipated mass on SATURDAY on a trial basis. If the manangs would complain then the Saturday Mass will stop. But as we went on the Priests saw that the community had been attuned to the modern tune and young ones had been flocking to our Mass. Napupuno ang church noon especially with the teenagers that were in attendance. Kaya nga the Priests decided to make the Saturday Mass a permanent one. That was how the JAZZ MASS CHOIR was born
